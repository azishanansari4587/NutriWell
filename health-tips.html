Health Tips

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Hydration supports digestion, skin health, and energy levels throughout the day.

🥗

Eat a Rainbow

Include colorful fruits and vegetables in every meal. Different colors mean different nutrients your body needs.

😴

Sleep Well

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep. Good sleep boosts immunity, improves mood, and sharpens focus.

🚶

Walk Daily

A 30-minute walk each day reduces risk of heart disease, improves mood, and strengthens bones and muscles.

🧘

Manage Stress

Practice meditation, deep breathing, or yoga. Chronic stress weakens immunity and impacts overall health significantly.

☀️

Get Sunlight

15-20 minutes of morning sunlight boosts vitamin D, improves mood, regulates sleep cycles and strengthens bones.
